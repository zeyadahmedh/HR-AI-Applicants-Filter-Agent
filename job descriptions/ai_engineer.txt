Job Title: AI Engineer

Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and deploy AI and machine learning models.
- Work with large datasets to extract meaningful insights.
- Collaborate with software engineers and data scientists to integrate AI solutions into products.
- Maintain and improve existing AI models and pipelines.
- Research and implement state-of-the-art AI algorithms and techniques.

Requirements:
- Strong knowledge of machine learning and deep learning techniques.
- Experience with Python and AI frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn.
- Familiarity with NLP, computer vision, or reinforcement learning.
- Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, and statistics.
- Experience with cloud platforms like AWS, GCP, or Azure is a plus.
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a collaborative environment.
